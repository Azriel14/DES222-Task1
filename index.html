THE FUTURE

OF OUR RELATIONSHIP WITH SPACES

HOW DO YOU DEFINE AND CHANGE YOUR RELATIONSHIPS TO PLACES? UNDERSTAND WHERE YOU ARE IN RELATION TO OTHER PLACES? HOW DO YOU UNDERSTAND THE PLACE YOU ARE IN? HOW DO YOU KNOW WHERE YOU ARE? SIGNAL WHERE YOU ARE?

NEARLY ALL TECH HAS THE CAPACITY OF ADAPTING TO ITS ENVIRONMENT (AUTO-BRIGHTNESS SCREENS, MOTION SENSORS, ETC.), BUT BETTER YET, WHAT IF YOUR TECH'S UNDERSTANDING OF ITS SURROUNDINGS ALLOWED IT TO PROVIDE YOU A SYNTHETICALLY ENHANCED EXPERIENCE. WHETHER IT'S VR, AR, OR MAYBE EVEN MR, WE'VE ALREADY ACHIEVED FORMS OF ALLOWING WHERE YOU ARE RIGHT NOW TO BE WAY MORE THAN YOU THINK IT'S CRACKED UP TO BE. THIS IS MY UNDERSTANDING WHEN PROMPTED WITH BEING IN PLACE: RELATIONSHIPS WITH SPACES IN TERMS OF TECHNOLOGY.

COMPANIES LIKE HAVENXR, FOUNDED IN 2019, IS ALREADY PIONEERING VR INTO ENTERTAINMENT BY TRANSPORTING USERS INTO CAPTIVATING VIRTUAL WORLDS. LEVERAGING CUTTING-EDGE VIRTUAL REALITY TECHNOLOGY WITH PHYSICAL SETS, HAVENXR OFFERS IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES THAT REDEFINE ENTERTAINMENT BY BLENDING IMAGINATION, INNOVATION, AND INTERACTIVITY.

TECH GIANTS LIKE APPLE ARE PUSHING THE BOUNDARIES OF WHAT IS POSSIBLE IN TERMS OF SPATIAL COMPUTING, AUGMENTED REALITY (AR), AND LOCATION-BASED SERVICES.

THE APPLE VISION PRO IS A HIGH-END VIRTUAL REALITY (VR) AND AUGMENTED REALITY (AR) HEADSET THAT WAS ANNOUNCED BY APPLE IN JUNE 2023. IT FEATURES TWO ULTRA-HIGH-RESOLUTION DISPLAYS, A POWERFUL PROCESSOR, AND A VARIETY OF SENSORS THAT ALLOW FOR IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES. THE APPLE VISION PRO IS A POWERFUL VR AND AR HEADSET THAT IS CAPABLE OF DELIVERING IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES. IT IS STILL IN DEVELOPMENT, BUT IT HAS THE POTENTIAL TO REVOLUTIONIZE THE WAY WE INTERACT WITH TECHNOLOGY.

THIS TECHNOLOGY STILL HAS THE CAPACITY TO GROW IN THE FUTURE, POSSIBLY LEADING TO ONE RESEMBLING THAT FOUND IN THE 2016 SHORT FILM "HYPER-REALITY", DIRECTED BY KEIICHI MATSUDA. THE FILM EXPLORES A FUTURE WHERE AUGMENTED REALITY HAS BECOME AN INTEGRAL PART OF DAILY LIFE. THE PROTAGONIST WEARS AR GLASSES THAT OVERLAY DIGITAL INFORMATION AND VIRTUAL OBJECTS ONTO THE PHYSICAL WORLD. THROUGHOUT THE FILM, THE PROTAGONIST ENCOUNTERS A SERIES OF SURREAL AND CHAOTIC SCENES. DIGITAL ADVERTISEMENTS AND INFORMATION CONSTANTLY BOMBARD THE PROTAGONIST'S VISION, CREATING A SENSE OF INFORMATION OVERLOAD.

THERE IS ALSO THE EVER-PRESENT CHANCE OF THE METAVERSE BEING WIDELY ADOPTED, WHICH COULD POTENTIALLY BRING ABOUT NOTABLE CHANGES IN HOW WE INTERACT WITH DIGITAL ENVIRONMENTS AND REDEFINE VARIOUS ASPECTS OF WORK, COMMUNICATION, AND LEISURE. META PROMISES THIS WOULD PROVIDE AN IMMERSIVE NATURE THAT OFFERS THE POSSIBILITY OF INFLUENCING A RANGE OF INDUSTRIES AND ACTIVITIES.
